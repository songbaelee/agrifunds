# Agricultural Investment Fund Ecosystem

An interactive map of capital flows in agricultural SME finance — showing who funds the funds, and where capital is being deployed.

Built as a working reference tool for practitioners in agri-SME lending and impact investing.

**[View the map →](https://agrifunds.songbae.com)**

## What it shows

- Capital relationships between investors and funds — DFIs, bilateral donors, foundations, and commercial investors
- Fund-level data: AUM, instrument type, tenor, average ticket size, geography, and return expectations
- Portfolio company data where publicly available
- Capital stack breakdowns for funds with disclosed tranche data

## Data

Data is sourced from fund websites, annual reports, GIIN, Convergence, and public press releases. Where key figures were not publicly available, proxies or estimates are used and noted. The dataset currently covers 13 funds and 48 investors.

Gaps and estimates are documented in `ecosystem_data.json`.

## Scope and limitations

Coverage is limited to funds with meaningful public disclosure. This is not a comprehensive market map — it is a starting point. Suggestions for funds to add are welcome.
